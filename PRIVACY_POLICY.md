# Privacy Policy for Smart Habit Coach

**Last Updated**: November 30, 2025

## Introduction

Smart Habit Coach ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

**Locally Stored Data**:
- Habit information (title, description, frequency, reminder times)
- Completion records and dates
- Streak calculations
- User preferences and settings

**Analytics Data** (Optional):
- App usage events (habit created, completed, etc.)
- Feature usage statistics
- Crash reports and error logs
- Device type and OS version (anonymized)

### What We DON'T Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone)
- Location data
- Contacts or photos
- Payment information
- Any data from other apps

## Data Storage

### Local Storage
All your habit data is stored **locally on your device** using AsyncStorage. This means:
- Your data never leaves your device
- No cloud servers store your information
- You have complete control over your data
- Uninstalling the app will delete all data

### Analytics Queue
If analytics is enabled:
- Events are queued locally first
- Synced only when you have internet connection
- All data is anonymized
- You can disable analytics anytime in Settings

## Data Usage

We use your data solely to:
- Display your habits and progress
- Calculate streaks and statistics
- Send you reminder notifications
- Generate weekly insights
- Improve app functionality (if analytics enabled)

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share, sell, or transfer your data to third parties.**

Your habit data remains private and local to your device.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Delete**: Remove individual habits or all data
- **Control**: Enable/disable analytics tracking
- **Export**: (Future feature) Export your data

## Permissions

### Required Permissions

**Notifications**:
- Purpose: Send habit reminders at scheduled times
- Control: Can be disabled in device settings

**Storage**:
- Purpose: Save your habits locally on device
- Control: Required for app functionality

### Optional Permissions

**Analytics**:
- Purpose: Improve app experience
- Control: Can be disabled in app Settings

## Children's Privacy

Smart Habit Coach does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and does not require age verification.

## Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:
- Local encryption of sensitive data
- No transmission of personal information
- Secure coding practices
- Regular security updates

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted within the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.

## Third-Party Services

### Expo Framework
The app is built using Expo, which may collect:
- Crash reports (if enabled)
- Performance metrics
- Device information

For Expo's privacy policy, visit: https://expo.dev/privacy

### Victory Native (Charts)
Used for displaying charts. No data is sent to external servers.

## Data Retention

- **Habit Data**: Stored until you delete it or uninstall the app
- **Analytics Events**: Cleared after successful sync or after 30 days
- **App Settings**: Stored until you reset or uninstall
